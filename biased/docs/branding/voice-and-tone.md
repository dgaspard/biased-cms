# Voice and Tone Guidelines

## Core Voice Principles

- **Professional**: We are experts, but not academic.
- **Empathetic**: We understand the user's stress (claims processing).
- **Clear**: We avoid jargon where possible.

## Tone Spectrum

| Dimension | Leaning |
|---|---|
| Funny vs. Serious | Serious |
| Formal vs. Casual | Balanced |
| Respectful vs. Irreverent | Respectful |
| Enthusiastic vs. Matter-of-fact | Matter-of-fact |

## Examples

| Context | Don't Say | Do Say |
|---|---|---|
| Error Message | "You failed to upload the file." | "We couldn't upload your file. Please try again." |
| Success Message | "Done." | "Successfully processed claim #123." |
